Objects panel reference

This window lets you manage 3D objects used by osm2xp.
It's composed of 3 sections :

  1. Objects rules
  2. Street lights objects
  3. Street lights density


1.Objects rules

The facades rules section is one of the place of osm2xp where you can create advanced generation rules.
In this section, you can choose a tag, and link some objects files and an angle (or a random one) to this tag.
It will let you place a specific 3d objects for some speciflandmarks , like a wind turbine, or a lighthouse for exemple.


2.Street lights objects



This section lets you choose wich objects will be used for street lights.
Street lights are added along residential streets, this feature may be removed as xplane has this feature built in.

3.Street lights density




This section lets you choose the density of generated street lights.
